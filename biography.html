A Little About Me

Hi, my name is Kai Brooks and I am a recent college graduate and UX Designer raised in the world of international humanitarian organizations. From a very young age I had a deep passion for helping others.

While obtaining my B.A in International Affairs I worked with CARE USA focusing my efforts in advocacy and human resources. At CARE I broadened my knowledge of international humanitarian issues as well as understanding people. This deepened my curiosity for the world and led me to pursue my studies abroad.

My studies abroad were focused on global politics, anthropology, and environmental sustainability. My passion for helping others intensified greatly. Upon my return home to Atlanta I discovered my passion for design. UX design allows me to add a positive impact in people’s lives. I love that I can use my creativity to find solutions. It is my passion to make the world more beautiful, one project at a time.
